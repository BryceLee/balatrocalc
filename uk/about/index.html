About balatrocalc

What is this site? Why build it?

balatrocalc is a Balatro hand score calculator and build helper. Configure your hand, enhancements, Jokers, upgrades, and modifiers to estimate the final score and understand where it comes from (via Breakdown).

Why it exists

  • Balatro scoring looks simple, but trigger order makes intuition unreliable.
  • Let the tool do the math so you can focus on decisions: what to buy, what to sell, and Joker order.
  • Make learning reproducible: verify ideas with clear breakdowns.
