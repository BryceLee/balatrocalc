Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how balatrocalc (“we”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit balatrocalc.com. We do not provide an account system and we do not ask for personal identity data like your name or phone number.

Information we collect

  • Usage data (analytics): we may use third-party analytics (e.g. Google Analytics) to collect aggregated statistics like page views, session duration, device/browser info, and approximate location. This helps improve the site.
  • Log data: hosting providers may log IP address, timestamps, requested pages, and error details for security and debugging.
  • Information you provide: if you contact us via Support, we use your email content/address to respond and handle the request.

Cookies and similar technologies

Third-party analytics may use cookies or similar technologies. You can block or clear cookies in your browser; this may affect analytics and some experience, but usually won’t affect the core calculator.

How we use information

  • Analyze usage and improve pages/content.
  • Debug issues and improve performance/security.
  • Respond to support requests.

Third-party services

The site may load third-party scripts/services (analytics, donation widgets, etc.). Those parties process data under their own privacy policies. We do not control their collection behavior, but we aim to minimize unnecessary collection.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above; exact retention may depend on hosting/third-party default settings.

Your choices

  • Limit cookies, block third-party scripts, or use privacy extensions.
  • If you want deletion of information you provided via email, contact support@balatrocalc.com (we’ll handle within reasonable scope).

Contact

If you have questions: support@balatrocalc.com

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates are posted on this page and take effect immediately.
